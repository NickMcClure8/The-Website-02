Directions

  1. Step 1: First, make a frame and add a grid. Simply type F, then select a device. For this, we will select an Iphone.
  2. Step 2: Now that we have our frame, we also want to choose a grid option to allow us to better allign are Mockup elements. Simply go over to the right side of Figma’ s UI, click the plus sign next to the grid, and change the grid to four columns. Also add a margin (i’m choosing 40px) to give our elements plenty of breathing room!s
  3. Step 3:Add basic elements and align them to the grid!
